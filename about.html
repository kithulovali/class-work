Our History & Heritage

Department Timeline

2009 - Foundation Year

Department of Informatics and Computing established with initial intake of 50 students in Computer Science program

2012 - Expansion

Launched Information Systems program and established first research lab for systems security

2015 - Infrastructure Development

Constructed modern computing labs with state-of-the-art infrastructure and equipment

2018 - Internationalization

Established partnerships with international universities and launched student exchange programs

2021 - Digital Transformation

Implemented hybrid learning model and launched digital transformation initiative for online education

2024 - Present Excellence

Department now serves 500+ students with 35+ faculty members and multiple research initiatives in AI, IoT, and Cloud Computing
