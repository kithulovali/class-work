Admission Requirements

Academic Requirements

  • Minimum Qualifications: Ugandan A-Level or equivalent international qualification
  • Mathematics: Grade C or better in Mathematics (Essential requirement)
  • English Language: Grade C or better in English Language
  • Science Subjects: At least one Science subject (Physics, Chemistry, or Biology)
  • GPA: Minimum 2.0 GPA (or equivalent) from secondary education

Required Documents

  • Original birth certificate or national ID
  • Original A-Level results and transcripts
  • Completed application form
  • Two recent passport-sized photographs
  • Recommendation letters from previous teachers (2)
  • Medical examination report
  • Police clearance certificate

English Language Requirements

International students whose first language is not English must provide:

  • TOEFL score (minimum 550 paper-based / 80 internet-based)
  • IELTS score (minimum 6.0 overall band)
  • Cambridge English Certificate (Grade B or better)

Scholarships & Financial Aid

Available Scholarships

Merit-Based Scholarship

Up to 50% tuition coverage for students with outstanding academic achievements. Covers full scholarship for top 3 applicants annually.

Need-Based Scholarship

Assistance for qualified students demonstrating financial need. Covers tuition, accommodation, and living expenses based on merit and need.

Regional Excellence Award

Recognition and partial tuition sponsorship for top performers from underrepresented regions in East Africa.

Industry Partnership Sponsorship

Fully funded scholarships from tech companies for promising students in IoT, AI, and Cloud Computing specializations.

Payment Plans

We offer flexible payment plans to accommodate different financial situations:

  • Full Payment: 10% discount on annual tuition
  • Semester Payment: Standard rate (2 payments per year)
  • Monthly Installments: Available with financial agreement
  • Work-Study Programs: Earn tuition credits through on-campus work
