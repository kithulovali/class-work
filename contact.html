Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for admission?

Visit our Admissions page for detailed requirements and application procedures.

Technical support is available Monday-Friday 8 AM - 5 PM. Emergency support available 24/7 for critical issues.

How can I contact my instructor?

Access the Staff directory for faculty contact information and office hours.

Are campus tours available?

Yes! Contact our Admissions office at admissions@bugema.ac.ug to schedule a campus tour.
